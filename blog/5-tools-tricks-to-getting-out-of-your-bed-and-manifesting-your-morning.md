If we are going to be *completely honest* right now. I currently have and am writing in more than **4 different journals** for a plethora with a different use for each of them. I have a continual use for journals and I feel like many other people with ADHD would agree. 

 

The use of a journal gives me the **tactile feel of expressing things down on paper** and it gives me the ability to **put my thoughts down without having to bottle them up**; which is **LIFE CHANGING**! One of these journals that I use is made completely for ideas and thoughts that I had in my head when I’m not in the *“happy go lucky”* kind of mood. It’s a **wreck it journal**, a journal where once it is written, **it is never looked at again**. 

 

I personally think that **there is nothing that writing cannot help**, let it be known that I also write for not only **therapeutic** reasons but as a **form of practice**. Because like you may or may not know. I am BOTH…yes, I mean both **ADHD and Dyslexia**. It’s the **equivalent of winning the neurological lottery.**

 

So yes, I write in the morning to give myself direction during the day, I use it as well to keep track of my finances, and other things that I would normally forget unless written down. But out of all the daily things that my journal reminds me of is my morning routine, MY morning routine, which is most important.

 

But this is me personally, and I think that writing could come in any way, shape, or form. It could be digital, in a journal, in a form of art. Whatever it may be that **gives you a way to express your emotions** **and give you a way to vent in any way**! 

 

I implore you to **do your own research and find out what fits you**. Once you do, share with me! I am in no place close to perfect and I want to learn just as much as everyone!
