At long last! The second part of this blog is live and ready for action. You are looking at a deliberate attempt at putting my *scattered thoughts* into words. Yes, I mean *scattered thoughts*. As many of you know I have **ADHD**, therefore, my brain is constantly *grabbing emotions*, *impulses*, thoughts float in as *the hyperactivity pulses*, and it’s part of that good stuff, which makes us: **“US”.** 

 

With that, this is the second part of a pretty insane and scary start. More specifically, it was **terrifying** for me to write.

 

I was scared to think of those events again and what they might do to me. I was terrified that they would seep into my mind during a heavy day at work, and I would lose motivation to do LITERALLY ANYTHING at all. Thus, ending productivity before even beginning. 

 

Once again, let me introduce who the **Arbitrary Author** is, the drive, the goals, the ideas, the rhymes, the rhythms of what makes me tick and what is to come next. 

 

So strap in your seat belts, turn up whatever the fuck you got play, and let’s get lost on the journey home. 



**So who the fuck is the Arbitrary Author?**

 

For those who don’t know: My name is **Benjamin Sklivas**, soon to be known as **The Arbitrary Author**. Damn, that sounds good off the tongue, doesn’t it? 

 

For non-English, **Arbitrary** is a word that means without reason or rhyme for doing LITERALLY ANYTHING, which I think is a great fit. Because let’s be serious real quick, I was introduced to the mindset of: *"Let’s go on an adventure!"* - by the one and only **Snow White**: and now it is a lifestyle choice. The mindset is more of a reflection of the power I am taking back from such a random word.

 

**The Arbitrary Author** has to be the most *erratic, impulsive, and hyperactive person*! If you know someone who embodies it more: please introduce them to me because we might have a new best friend lined up (Sorry J if you’re reading this). So slide up on my DMs, and chat! Because TBH as much fun and appealing this growth stage of my life is…im still lowkey lonely.

 

Back to where I was going before I regularly spaced out while writing-

 

The reason that I created "**The Arbitrary Author**" was to **solidify change**, a **rebrand of sorts** after all that has happened in the last year. I know that you all know what I’m talking about; **Loss of Job, Loss of Freedom, Loss of Self, and last but not least, the loss of what I thought I needed; snow white.**

 

This blog post is my introduction, and I will be moving on to **evolve my brand**, **grow my presence, giving back to the people that need it**, and **tell my story.**

 

This will be a reflection on the person that *I was*, meditations on *whom I want to* be rather than feeling like I need to *revert to the person that I was before*.

 

I want to prove people **wrong**, **I do not care what they think of me since it would defeat the purpose**. This is for me, but I really hope you can stay and enjoy the ride.

 

Due to a Global Pandemic all events and gatherings will be postponed, all activities, also be postponed. Shit! Well if our *COVID era* of staying inside has helped me realize something, it would be that I have a stockpile of **creative juices flowing through me with this being my only outlet**!

 

I never realized that The Arbitrary Author could be used to my advantage and at the same time help others grow their brand own brands without depending on others. **SO WEBINAR AND SUMMIT EVENTS TO COME?**!

 

So screw the narrative of *“an eye for an eye, leave the world blind”* and move forward with **EVE – EVERYBODY LOVE EVERYBODY.**



**SECRET TIME!!**

 

I woke up one morning, which was probably around month one of being single. Woke up, went to the bathroom and looked at me in the mirror. I had cried myself out of bed, I was trying to get myself ready for a **HUGE** call with potentially the biggest sale of my life! 

 

There I was. **Fucking crying in the bathroom**. And it hit me, and that was that I was done feeling pity, I was done believing what others had been calling me and just done putting myself last all the time.



*I was done being the cheater, the liar, the bad friend, and it was time for me to move on and be better!*

 

Fast forward………**BOOM**, friends are reaching out asking to help me grow my site, my brand, my interactions with the public. At this point**, I know that I have the support of my everyone in my life.** But now it comes time to see what everyone else thinks.

 

**Let me know if this is a colossal fuck up or if you think I’m moving in the right direction!** 

 

I could not be any happier for the direction that my life took, minus maybe the whole- you know - losing everything and crying myself to sleep for around 2 months. **My mindset had changed**; I even took **ACID** for the first time with my roommate with the intention of: *“if this was the right thing for me to do, If I was meant to be going in the direction, then life would take me there.”* I was done following road maps and being told what to do..

 

**We came out of a 12-hour long roller coaster** ride where my friends magically appeared into existence, in my living room, as my roommate I were just staring off into the wood grain of a bedroom door. The ideas that we could realistically line for line paint the Mona Lisa or that we could have seriously beaten the world record of Jenga (This is no joke, we actually came SUPER CLOSE)

 

So yeah, I realize that I **fucked up**. I realize that I fucked up **BADLY**. But try and understand that I am in a place where I have acknowledged my fuck ups and am now in the process of growing through them. So, this is the last time that you will hear me apologize, because that is no longer who I am.

 

But for real, my aim for **2021 is to give you an unfiltered version of my life that you can either love or hate**. Because honestly at this point in my life **I could care less about the hate that I receive daily**. I just want to give back, I want to help, I want  to grow without alteriar motives.

 

**Instead of playing pretend and feeding into this toxic positivity and fake friends bullshit**: **Let’s be honest about shit hitting the fan**, let’s be honest about our **mental health,** and whatever else you’re going through. so more than anything let’s just be genuine to one another for once shall we?

 

Before I can move on from this part of the post to **the next big chapter** and what I aim to do! I think what needs to be cleared up is that **I could not have done this without the people closest to me**.

 

I was lost, but my friends family and the people closest to me always made me feel *supported*. It’s for that reason that I am proud to announce **my goals for the next year**



**THE NEW CHAPTER: Transparency, Honesty, Hard Work, Personal Growth, Mental Health and more.**

 

 

The new chapter: Coffee Break. That’s all I really got right now. All I am thinking of right now is the intro song for **Pokémon** (you know the old school 90s theme!?).

But now that *I am on my own*, working for myself with an amazing team, positivity on the brain (in a completely non-toxic way).
